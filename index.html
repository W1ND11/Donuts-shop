Welcome to Bethany's Donuts Shop

Our history

For many years, Bethany has been baking the most delicious Donuts at her home. In 2013, we opened our first store in Brussels (Belgium) in a cosy little street near the Grand Market. People from all over the world fell in love with the soft Donuts, heavenly cheese cakes, spicy apple pies... The store became a success in no time.
In 2015, no less than 300 Donuts were sold every day and Bethany's Donuts Shop moved into a larger building. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order Donuts from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this: our very own webstore, making it possible to order Bethany's delicious Donuts whenever you feel like it!

Bethany is famous for using only the best ingredients. This way, we can make sure that our donuts are absolutely the best.
